To view my js script solution, see below:


Windows users:
a) Keyboard shortcut - Press the Control+Shift+i keys simultaneously to open the console directly.
b) Right click and select inspect and view console.

Mac users:
a) Keyboard shortcut - Press the Command+Option+j keys simultaneously to open the console directly.
b) To open the developer tools, press Command+Option+i, and click on the Console tab in the opened window.
